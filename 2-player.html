Lost Cities

The game Lost Cities is a great 2 player game by Reiner Knizia. It is elegant in its simplicity. It is, however, a brutal game that will have you cursing your opponent. Me and my wife have fun playing this, even though she regularly trounces me! There is lots of "push your luck" in this game, so beware if you are a fan of "perfect information".

Fox in the Forest

Fox in the Forest is a lovely 2 player trick taking game. And they said it couldn't be done! It mixes special powers on the odd cards with a strange scoring system to create a lot of tension throughout each hand and the whole game overall. The special abilities are easy to remember, eventhough they are written on the cards. You have to always be flexible and willing to change your strategy on a dime. It can be a very tight competition between players. I used to think I was quite good at this game until my brother beat me solidly the first two times he played. I still love it though.

7 Wonders Duel

This adaptation of the modern classic 7 Wonders to two players is considered superior to its predecessor by many. It is highly ranked on BGG, and for good reason. This tableau builder sees you working through 3 different ages with progressively more powerful cards. The main hurdle in devloping a two player variant was in how to implement drafting for only two players. This was masterfully done by Bruno Cathala, and gave him the reputation of being the king of 2 player variants. There are many roads to victory and different special abilities throughout the game. One tip, don't underestimate science!
